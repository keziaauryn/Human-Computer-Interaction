Welcome to Xtream Chaser

Xtream Chaser is a fighting game developed by Cold Fox Company and was released in 2020.

The main games is the events of Fire Battle Tournament, where the players control the characters to reach the battle with Lord Gora, the defending champion of Fire Battle Tournament.

Gameplay focuses on hand-to-hand combat with an opponent, with many moves selection such as hit, block, throw, and ground fighting. There are several mode in this game which were Team battle, Time attack, and Practice.
